Hi there

 

 

    Guess what! Pehia is back with a newer version of the annual celebration - Pehia Summit, on January 26th 2021 and you are invited! With all due respect to the social situations we face, we are conducting the summit online this year, but with no quality compromised.

 

As you would know, Pehia is a non-commercial organization working to realize the efforts to reduce the gender gap and increase the inclusiveness in the field of technology. Pehia Summit is the annual celebration of the success stories that have smashed the stereotypes and bridged the invisible distance in all the paths that one has to take in daily life. And now, we want you to be a part of the same, taking the best of what you seek, and polish yourselves to a new year.

 

This year, Pehia Summit will have 2 technical tracks. 

       Track 1

Open source- you learn about how to start open-source contribution, do some fun hands-on session.

       Track 2

Let us Build some cool stuff! - you learn about competitive coding and later build products during the hands-on session.

 

 

 

The summit will witness some of the most powerful, coolest and fun speakers who can inspire you and answer the questions that must have been ringing in your mind for a while.

 

Well, its always better to warm up your thoughts before the summit, for which we present 'Code In: To the Summit' a Hacker rank challenge spanning over 4 rounds of coding questions. You win goodies while you win the rounds-( we've got you covered!). Also, we provide you with the opportunity to receive one-on-one mentorship from the most experienced and helpful resources and industry specialists in the field of your aptitude. 

 

Let us begin the year with the right start!

 

Do reach out and write back to us for any queries you need to get off your mind.

What are you waiting for?
